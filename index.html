About Hypercube

Hypercube is a Meetup group in Pasadena, California. This group is geared towards anyone who uses technology as a means of human expression -- creative coders, musicians, sound designers, animators, graphic designers, performers, game developers, etc are all welcome to join!

Technology not only makes our lives easier, but it allow us to creatively express ourselves in ways that was previously impossible. Now, human expression have expanded past physical media and have taken a more digital form. Generative arts, mathematical algorithms, musical-sequencers/drum-machines, code, Arduninos, and computer software have become the modern tools for digital artists to develop meaningful creative experiences using technology.
