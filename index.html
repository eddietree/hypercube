About Us

Hypercube is a Meetup group geared towards anyone who is using technology to create art -- creative coders, musicians, designers, performers, game developers, etc are all welcome!

Technology not only makes our lives easier, but it also can be used creatively to allow us to express ourselves in a way that was previously impossible. Generative arts, algorithms, code, musical-sequencers/drum-machines, and computer software have become the modern tools to develop meaningful creative experiences.

Organized by Eddie Lee
Emails to eddie@hypercube.io

Meetup

Every month, we all get together somewhere in Pasadena to hang out and talk about games, art and tech. Anyone who has an interest in creative coding is welcome to swing by.
